# Drift

> *find your people*

Drift is a social media app built around real friendships and healthy usage, not engagement at all costs.

---

## The Problem

TikTok, Instagram, and Snapchat are all designed to maximize time spent. The algorithm rewards popularity over quality, follower counts create a "rich get richer" dynamic, and there's no separation between your close friends and the content machine.

Drift fixes that.

---

## What Makes Drift Different

### Fair Feed
Content is served chronologically, not by follower count or algorithm. A creator with 10 followers has the same shot as someone with 100k. Your reach is based on whether people actually like your content, not how big you already are.

### Friends Are Unlimited
Mutual friends and people you mark as close friends are always unlimited. You will never miss a post from someone you actually care about. The daily limit only applies to creator and discovery content.

### Daily Digest Limit
You choose how much outside content you consume each day, by post count or by time. Increments of 5, fully optional, always adjustable in settings. Creators count toward your limit. Friends never do.

### The Catch-Up Feed
When you hit your daily limit, instead of hitting a wall, Drift shows you a scrollable section of unseen friend posts in chronological order oldest to newest. No algorithm, no creators, just the stuff from your people you might have missed. Like early Facebook, before it became an infinite scroll machine.

### No Public Follower Count in the Algorithm
Follower counts are visible but never used to determine reach. Popularity doesn't feed itself here.

### User or Creator
During onboarding you choose your mode. Regular users get the friend-first experience. Creators get tools to grow their audience but on a level playing field.
